Bangkok Buddha Bar




The first PFP collection from acrylic paintings on THETA


Bangkok Buddha Bar is the first generative profile picture collection made from acrylic paintings on the Theta blockchain. 444 Buddhas have been created from over 40 physical fine art paintings on canvas to illuminate the blockchain.


Bridging fine art and generative art


In a certain way BBB can be seen as a bridge between traditional fine art and recent generative NFT art. Our community aims to support traditional artists who are new to the NFT space.

Elevate human consciousness


Our goal is to contribute to global transformation. Let us create a world full of love, happiness and laughter.

15% of the earnings will be donated to organizations that work on lifting up human consciousness. Holders of a BBB NFT will decide how the fund should be used to accomplish the aforementioned.

Free Genesis 2 NFT


In addition holders of our Genesis 1 collection will be airdropped one genesis 2 NFT for free. Our Genesis 2 collection will be created from about 60 to 80 fine art paintings and launch later in 2022.
